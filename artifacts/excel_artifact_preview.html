ETH Price Analysis Overview

This Excel project analyzes Ethereum (ETH) price trends over a 10-year period, including open, high, low, and close values. The data is summarized into a separate table that calculates minimum, median, maximum, and average prices per year.

The line chart visualizes price evolution over time, helping spot growth trends and volatility. The structure of the workbook demonstrates:

  • Effective use of Excel formulas including MIN, MEDIAN, and AVERAGE
  • Clear visual formatting and conditional styling
  • Data sorting, filtering, and use of calculated fields

This sheet would be valuable in a financial analyst role, crypto market research, or historical trend reporting.
