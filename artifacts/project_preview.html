Overview

This project followed PMI-aligned practices to simulate a housing development initiative. Deliverables were created in MS Project and Excel, tracking tasks, scope, risks, and costs from start to finish.

Key Focus Areas

  • Scope & Initiation: Defining objectives and constraints
  • Planning: Developing a Requirements Matrix and managing stakeholders
  • Execution: Visualizing project dependencies through a network diagram
  • Risk Management: Documenting and prioritizing potential risks
  • Cost Analysis: Tracking budget performance through variance and dashboards
